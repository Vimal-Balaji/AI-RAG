Halfwave Rectifier.
AC Mains $\rightarrow$ Step Down Transformer $\rightarrow$ Diode $\rightarrow R_L$.
Rectifier: It converts AC signal into DC Signal.
During the Positive Half cycle, the Diode comes under forward bias, which results in the output same as the input.
During the Negative Half cycle, the Diode comes under reverse bias, which results in zero output.
